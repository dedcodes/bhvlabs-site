About

BHV Labs is the software product arm of Blue Horizon Ventures, LLC, a New Jersey limited liability company. We design, build, and operate consumer-facing applications for individuals who want better tools for managing their health, finances, and daily decisions.

Our work focuses on building software that is honest about data, respectful of user attention, and grounded in the way people actually live, rather than how technology companies wish they did.

Areas of Focus

  • Health & Nutrition
    Tools that help individuals make informed food and lifestyle decisions in real-world contexts.
  • Personal Finance
    Software that gives individuals clearer visibility into their financial decisions.
  • Productivity
    Applications designed to reduce cognitive load for high-agency professionals.
  • Personal Data
    Tools that put individuals in control of their own information.
